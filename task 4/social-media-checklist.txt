Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s: 1. Use strong passwords 2. Keep your profile professional 3. Share
useful and positive content 4. Respect others online 5. Verify
information before posting

Don’ts: 1. Don’t share personal information like OTP or passwords 2.
Don’t use abusive or offensive language 3. Don’t click on unknown links
4. Don’t spread fake news 5. Don’t waste too much time on social media
